How to use this page

Hey, welcome! In this page you'll be able to compare pilots that were competing in a given career from the years 2018 to 2022.

Please, read these steps before comparing pilots:

  • Step 1: Select a year, you can choose from 2018 to 2022.
  • Step 2: Once you have selected the year, you'll be able to see the races from that year, choose one from the second dropdown.
  • Step 3: Once you have selected the race, the pilots that participated in that race will be enabled in the third and fourth dropdown. Make sure you select a different pilot in each dropdown
